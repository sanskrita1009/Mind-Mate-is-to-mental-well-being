Basic Hygiene Manners

Hygiene refers to practices and conditions that help maintain health and prevent the spread of diseases. Good hygiene includes regular handwashing, proper food handling, safe water usage, and personal cleanliness.

Basic Mathematics

Basic mathematics encompasses fundamental arithmetic skills such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. It also includes understanding numbers, counting, and basic concepts of geometry and measurement.

Science

Science is the systematic study of the natural world through observation and experimentation. It encompasses disciplines such as biology, chemistry, physics, and earth sciences. Science education fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. It helps students understand how the world works, from the smallest particles to the vastness of space, and prepares them for a range of careers in research, technology, and medicine.

Art and Craft

Art and craft involve creative activities that allow individuals to express themselves and develop fine motor skills. These activities include drawing, painting, sculpting, and various handcrafts like knitting, origami, and pottery.
